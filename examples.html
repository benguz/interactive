CREATIVITY: Artists believe AI can sabotage or enable creativity depending on its use.

COMPENSATION: Dilution of the royalty pool, degradation of the value of work, lack of fair compensation, and the undermining of rights holders is echoed throughout the letter.

Is it all bad?

Music has been used at various stages of music creation, production, and distribution throughout the past several decades. Many of AI vocal-cloning’s determinants have been listed above and are important to reckon with as copyright legislation is updated.
